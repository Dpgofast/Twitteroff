TwitterOff!

Are you tired of adding users to your Twitter feed only to find out you aren't really interested in what they Tweet about? With Twitteroff you can fiter through users probable tweets without going through the trouble of following them only to unfollow them shortly after. Simply add 2 twitter usernames and input a sample tweet you would like to see in your feed. Our model will run all the previous tweets from each user and tell you who is more likely to tweet about the things that interests you!
